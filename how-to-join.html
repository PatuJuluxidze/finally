How to Join the RL

It is really quite simple, and there are many way of IDing yourself as a member. I will give you guys the steps to join, then I will tell you guys the ways you can ID yourself as a member.

  • Emali Astirrus with something along the lines of "Hey! I want in on this!"
  • Make sure that Astirrus emails you back saying "Your in."
  • Use an Identifier to mark you as a member

By following those steps you can now see the Identifiers that you can use. They vary from game to game, but this is a very easy thing to do.

  • Halo: In the four letter/digit short name you can give yourself in multiplayer, make the first to numbers RL.
  • CSGO: In the Guild Card comment, enter "I got your back." or "Anneux!" and send it to a member.
  • Roblox: Be friends with all members that play.
  • PUBG game: Before you enter your name, put [RL] in first.

    • After entering the Identifier, you are now an offical member. Now try and climb the ranks!
