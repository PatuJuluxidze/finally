Team Play

This is where we will be showing you team play and giving some tips as to how to go about it. It will be broken up ito a few sections that will help you to become a master at team play. Disclaimer: We are not, by any means, saying that we are team play experts. We just want to help anyone who may be a solo player or someone who hates people, yet plays a team oriented game.

Communication

Communication is key. I don't call communication: "Hey, how are you doing?" "Oh, I'm ok. How about you?" No, just NO! That is SO wrong that it makes me sick when I hear that in AirMech. It is more like: "Hey, how's your HP?" "It's kinda low... Mind healing me while I go take the objective? I can shield you from blows" Now THAT'S more like it. Communication is all about giving your status, calling enemies, and calling attacks or retreats. If you don't do that, then I really can't help but not feel bad when your team is absolutely DOMINATED.

Scouting

Scouting is the process of figuring out your opponent(s) skill level(s) before you go up to them and beat them up. For instance, if you were in a Vainglory/League of Legends tournament, you would probably draft characters that you can play well AND are good against what your opponents have drafted. YOu can also make eliminations of characters based on your opponent(s) skill level with some characters.
